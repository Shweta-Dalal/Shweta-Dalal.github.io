Research

A list of all the publications can also be found in the NASA ADS library

Ongoing projects:

1. Predicting Convective Blueshift of FGK stars and computing their RV dispersion due to granulation.

2. I am leading the radial velocity (RV) analysis of one of the Rocky Planet Search (RPS) targets.

Past Projects

Obliquity Measurements

1. I worked on HD3167 c, a warm sub-Neptune, which is a part of multi-planet system HD3167, for which we measured the sky-projected obliquity with HARPS-N spectrograph data for the first time.

2. I have also worked on the new SPIRou spectropolarimeter data (CFHT) to measure the obliquity of HD189733 b in the near-infrared wavelength domain.

Detecting and Characterizing Exoplanets

I worked on two exoplanet programs with the SOPHIE spectrograph at OHP, which detect giant planets and brown dwarfs, respectively. We detected a wealth of new objects which includes a dozen giant planets, three brown dwarf candidates, and sixteen low-mass binary stars under these programs.
